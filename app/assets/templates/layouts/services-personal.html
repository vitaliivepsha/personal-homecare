Personal Care Services & Companionship

back to Services

Our caregivers provide support and companionship for all of the ordinary things that happen for your loved one throughout the day — with extraordinary compassion and skill.

  • Help with Feeding & Eating — Our caregivers will help get your loved one ready for the day so that they look and feel their best.

  • Bathing & Dressing — From reminders to assistance, we provide the support that is needed.

  • Hygiene Assistance Toileting & Incontinence Care — A neat and tidy home helps us all feel better! Our caregivers help your loved one keep their home clean and organized.

  • Light Housekeeping — A neat and tidy home helps us all feel better! Our caregivers help your loved one keep their home clean and organized.

  • Laundry and change of bed linens — Getting into a clean bed helps all of us sleep better! Our caregivers will ensure your loved one has this handled for them.

  • Grocery Shopping — Our caregivers will help your loved ones acquire and put away their groceries.

  • Socializing & Outings — From visits with their friends and family to social outings, we can provide the assistance your loved one needs.

  • Mobility Assistance — Whether your loved one uses a cane, walker or wheelchair, we can provide the helping hand they need to get around safely and independently.

  • Transportation & Errands — Our caregivers will escort your loved one to doctors’ appointments, banks, the pharmacy, and medical supply stores.

  • Overnight Supervision & Live-In 24/7 Care — At A+ Home Care, we customize our schedule to the needs of your loved one. Live-in care helps your loved one remain comfortably and safely at home and lets them create a strong bond with their caregiver who they will see week after week.
