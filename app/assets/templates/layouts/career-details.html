Full Job Description

Are you a Caregiver looking for an exciting opportunity?
Well, the time is now – come grow with us!

Salim Inc. A Plus Personal Home Care is looking for experienced Caregivers to provide service to its personal care services in client’s homes.
We offer competitive compensation, flexible scheduling, opportunity for growth, and a positive work environment.

Duties

  • Assists clients with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) or Instrumental Activities of Daily Living (IADLs).

  • Activities of Daily Living may include, but are not limited to, bathing and personal hygiene, assistance with dressing, eating, mobility, and toileting.

  • Instrumental Activities of Daily Living may include, but are not limited to, meal preparation and planning, light housekeeping, errands, accompaniment to appointments, and assistance with communication such as the use of the telephone.

Requirements:

  • 1-2 years of caregiver experience

  • CNA/CMT (Preferred)

  • health certificate/physical examination and TB testing.

  • CPR & First Aide certification from an approved training source.

  • Clean criminal background screening.

  • Your own Vehicle or Reliable transportation.
