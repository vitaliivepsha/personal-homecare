Health & Wellness Services

back to Services

Our objective is to keep your loved one as healthy and well as possible. We prioritize everything that will help them do so.

  • Monitoring Health & Changing Needs— As things change, we keep a watchful eye and stay in regular communication with you so that your loved one’s needs can be quickly addressed as they evolve. This includes visits every 3-4 months from our Registered Nurses (RNs) who supervise, observe and evaluate your loved one’s ongoing care. Our RNs also directly support patients, their caregivers and their family members in assuring the best care possible.

  • Medication Reminders/Administration— We can help you determine the nutritious foods your loved one needs for optimum health.

  • Nutrition Assessment— Our caregivers will plan and prepare your loved one’s meals to adhere to medical recommendations and your preferences.

  • Meal Planning and Preparation— Having a caregiver present allows your loved one to move safely throughout their home with support as needed.

  • Safety & Fall Prevention— Having a caregiver present allows your loved one to move safely throughout their home with support as needed.

  • Self-Care Education & Referrals— Our caregivers are invested in helping your loved one learn to take better care of themselves, and also letting you know if there’s an opportunity for better care through the referral of another professional.

  • Appointment Assistance— For any and all appointments, our caregivers can serve as your loved one’s go-to support system.
