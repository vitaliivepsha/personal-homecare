Transitional Care

back to Services

If one thing is true in life it’s that things are always changing. At A+ Home Care, we will be there to support you and your loved one through all of the transitions that may come along.

  • Dressing Changes & Wound Care Assistance — You can have peace-of-mind knowing we’ll keep your loved one’s wounds clean and cared for so they can heal!

  • Injury & Illness Recovery — Getting better doesn’t just require all of a person’s physical needs being met, but their emotional needs too. Our caregivers bring smiles and compassion which speed the recovery process.

  • Prescription Pick-Up — If they need it, we’ll pick it up!

  • Accompaniment to Appointments Incidental Transportation — We will get your loved one to their appointments on time and provide the support they need.

  • Hospital Discharge Assistance — With comfort, health and safety as the top priorities, our caregivers can help your loved one recover after a hospital stay and reduce the risk of rehospitalization.
