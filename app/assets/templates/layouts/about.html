Why a+ home care
We provide personalized, compassionate, attentive care for your loved one … in their own home.

A+ Personal Home Care is an independent home care agency owned by Salim, Inc., a family-owned company based in Pikesville and serving the central Maryland and Baltimore Washington metro areas.

What we do

With our team of credentialed, screen, professional and compassionate caregivers, we offer elder care companion, personal care, homemaker, and home health aide services.

Our clients include seniors, disabled and cognitively impaired adults, and short and long-term medical recovery patients — anyone who needs a helping hand at home.
